French Toast Recipe

This french toast can be paired with toppings of your choice, such as fruit.

Materials for this recipe include a measuring cup, a bowl, a skillet, and a stovetop. The ingredients are as follows:
  • 1 cup of soy or almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons of chickpea flour
  • 2 tablespoons of nutritional yeast
  • 1 tablespoon of maple syrup or date honey
  • Â½ teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of coconut oil
  • pinch of cinnamon
  • 6 thick slices of bread
  • maple syrup

How to make:

  1. Add the chickpea flour to a shallow bowl, and add just a dash of the soy or almond milk.
  2. Mix until smooth, and then add the rest of the milk.
  3. Add the nutritional yeast, maple syrup, vanilla extract, salt, and cinnamon, and whisk together.
  4. Heat up a frying pan with a little coconut oil, and place on medium heat.
  5. Soak each slice of bread in the mixture for a few seconds on each side.
  6. Place slices onto the frying pan to cook until browned on both sides.
  7. Add your desired toppings, and drizzle with syrup.
